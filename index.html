About Me

I am a Software Engineer based in San Gabriel, California. I have experience with a variety of technologies, including React, Redux, JavaScript, Python, Flask, and more. I am a problem solver who thrives on challenges. I use analytical thinking and inventive strategies to consistently achieve success.

My unique journey has led me from a career in addiction treatment to a passion for coding and software development. My background in addiction treatment has given me a deep understanding of individuals facing adversity, and has nurtured my empathetic approach. While I was deeply committed to my work, a personal tragedy disrupted my ability to provide the level of care I aspired to deliver and brought on new challenges with social anxiety. This pivotal moment forced me to find a new path.

I began my journey into coding with personal exploration, which revealed a newfound passion. Software engineering is a fascinating combination of creation, evolution, and overcoming obstacles, which is a formula that I find very appealing. Recognizing the endless possibilities within coding, I sought structured learning to translate this passion into a thriving career.

I am motivated by the chance to create real-world solutions and make a positive impact in the world and I am excited to embrace new connections, embark on collaborations, and seize growth opportunities. If you are looking for a dedicated software engineer who brings technical expertise, empathy, and a passion for making a difference, I would be thrilled to connect and collaborate.
